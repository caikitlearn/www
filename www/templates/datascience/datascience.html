Hot Hub Time Machine

My Insight Data Science project for users of metro-Boston's bike-sharing system Hubway. Users enter their origin and destination, and the page produces walking directions to the Hubway station closest to the origin, biking directions to the Hubway station closest to the destination, and walking directions to the destination. A prediction of the number of available bikes at the Hubway stations near the origin and destination is accessible by clicking map icons. This allows users to plan ahead if a starting station might have no available bikes, or if an ending station might be too full to accept additional bikes.
